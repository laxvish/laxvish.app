## Core Products

### 1. Workers (AI Agents)
- **Description:** Specialized workers built for domain execution
- **Tagline:** Domain AI agents for real operations
- **Details:**
  - Role-specialized design - Each worker is scoped to a business function for predictable behavior
  - Composable activation - Workers can be combined into larger operational pipelines through Brain
  - Operational controls - Critical decisions can be gated before final actions are executed

### 2. Brain (AI Orchestration)
- **Description:** Coordinate workers with centralized intelligence
- **Tagline:** Orchestration and control intelligence
- **Details:**
  - Flow orchestration - Define workflow pathways and escalation branches in one control layer
  - Policy-aware routing - Actions are routed based on business rules, risk profile, and confidence
  - Observability built-in - Track bottlenecks, retries, and completion behavior for each workflow

### 3. Brakes (AI Verification)
- **Description:** Ship AI with brakes, not blind trust
- **Tagline:** Verification and governance safeguards
- **Details:**
  - Pre-action verification - Validate AI outputs before they touch customer-facing or regulated systems
  - Risk-triggered controls - Escalate uncertain outcomes to human review when thresholds are crossed
  - Audit-grade traces - Preserve explainable execution paths for governance and compliance reviews

### 4. CallMe (AI Voice Agent)
- **Description:** Realtime voice AI for enterprise conversations
- **Tagline:** AI voice agent for enterprise conversations
- **Details:**
  - Realtime voice - Natural speech recognition and generation for fluid, human-like conversations
  - Enterprise controls - Call recording, compliance logging, escalation paths, and policy enforcement
  - Workflow integration - Trigger actions, update records, and hand off to human agents seamlessly
